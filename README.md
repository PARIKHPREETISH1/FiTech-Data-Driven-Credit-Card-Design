## ðŸ§  Solution Approach
This project applies a structured analytics and product strategy framework:

1. **Customer Segmentation**
   - Group customers based on spending patterns, income levels, and usage behavior
   - Identify high-value and under-served segments

2. **Spending & Behavior Analysis**
   - Analyze transaction categories and frequency
   - Understand key drivers of card usage and churn

3. **Product Feature Design**
   - Design reward structures aligned with customer spending habits
   - Recommend credit limits, cashback categories, and benefits

4. **Business Impact Evaluation**
   - Assess potential revenue, engagement, and retention impact
   - Balance customer incentives with cost and risk considerations
